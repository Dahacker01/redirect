Captain Moroni

Defender of Liberty & Faith

A tribute to Captain Moroni, remembered in the Book of Mormon for his courage, leadership, integrity, and unwavering devotion to protecting his people and their freedoms.

"In Memory of Our God, Our Religion, and Freedom"

The Legacy

⚔ Courage

Captain Moroni stood firm in times of conflict and inspired others through strength, conviction, and determination.

🛡 Leadership

He united his people around shared principles and demonstrated leadership rooted in service and responsibility.

🏛 Liberty

The Title of Liberty became a symbol of freedom, faith, family, and the preservation of righteous principles.

“If all men had been, and were, and ever would be, like unto Moroni, behold, the very powers of hell would have been shaken forever.”

The Title of Liberty

📜 A Banner of Purpose

Moroni raised a standard to remind his people of the ideals they cherished and were willing to defend.

✨ A Symbol of Unity

The banner rallied communities together around faith, liberty, and commitment to one another.

🔥 An Enduring Inspiration

Generations have looked to the story of the Title of Liberty as a reminder of courage and devotion to noble causes.

Dedicated to Captain Moroni — a symbol of courage, faith, and liberty.
